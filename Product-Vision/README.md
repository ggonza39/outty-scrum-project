## Far Vision
Outty aims to become the leading adventure-focused matching platform that connects outdoor enthusiasts worldwide based on shared activities, experience levels, and exploration goals. The long-term vision is to build a trusted community where users can safely discover compatible adventure partners, form meaningful relationships, and explore the outdoors together across regions and cultures.

## Near Vision
In the near term, Outty will deliver a functional prototype that allows users to:
- Create and manage an adventure-based profile
- Select preferred outdoor activities and skill levels
- Discover and match with nearby users through a swipe-based interface
- Communicate with matched users to plan outings

The near vision focuses on validating core matching functionality, usability, and engagement before expanding features or scale.

## Stakeholders
- Primary users (outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers)
- Product owner and development team
- Course instructors and evaluators
- Potential future partners (outdoor brands, guides, event organizers)
